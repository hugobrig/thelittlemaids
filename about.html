Our Story

We Started Because Mediocre Cleaning Services Were Driving Us Crazy

You know the type — shows up late, misses the corners, leaves your house smelling like regret and that weird industrial cleaner. We'd had enough.

The Little Maids was built on a simple idea: Utah homeowners deserve a cleaning service that's actually reliable, actually thorough, and actually worth the money. Not a mystery, not a gamble — just a consistently clean house, every time.

We operate remotely so we can manage quality across Utah without the overhead of a brick-and-mortar location. That keeps costs down and standards up. Win-win.

Our Mission

Life is too short to spend it scrubbing grout. You've got kids to wrangle, hobbies to ignore, and Netflix queues that aren't going to watch themselves.
