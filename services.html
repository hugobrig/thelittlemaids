FAQ

Questions People Actually Ask

Do you bring your own supplies?

Yes. We bring everything — professional-grade products and equipment. You don't need to supply a single thing except access to your home.

How long does a clean take?

Depends on the size and type of service. A standard clean for a 2-bedroom home typically runs 2–3 hours. Deep cleans take longer. We'll give you an estimate when you book.

Do I need to be home?

Nope! Most clients give us a key or entry code and come home to a sparkling house. That's honestly the best part.

What if I'm not happy with my clean?

Tell us within 24 hours. We'll come back and fix whatever needs fixing, free of charge. That's our Little Maids Promise — no fine print, no drama.

How do I pay?

Securely online when you book. No awkward cash handoffs, no invoices to chase down. Easy.

Not Sure Which Service You Need?

Start with a Deep Clean if it's your first time with us. Then we can dial in a Standard Clean schedule from there. Easy.
